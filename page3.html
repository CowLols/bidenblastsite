Biden Bolt

Biden will use his bolt move if he wishes to pursue a target. When using this move he can travel at speeds close to 300 MPH. Biden can increase his speed by consuming an Ice Cream or by drinking an Ice Cold Beer. There is no known way to outrun the Biden Bolt. After using the Biden Bolt, Joe will take 2 minutes to recharge before he is able to attack again, while in this state Biden is vulnerable and can take damage.
